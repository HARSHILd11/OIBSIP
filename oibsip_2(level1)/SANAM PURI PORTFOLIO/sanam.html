About Sanam Puri

Sanam Puri is a famous Indian pop-rock band known for their soulful melodies, electrifying performances, and mesmerizing vocals. From their viral YouTube covers to their original compositions, Sanam Puri has won the hearts of millions of fans worldwide.

MUSIC

Sanam Puri's music is a blend of pop, rock, and Bollywood. They create original compositions that are soulful, uplifting, and easy on the ears. Their covers of Bollywood classics are a testament to their ability to add a unique touch to popular songs while staying true to the original melody..

LIVE Performances

Sanam Puri is known for their electrifying live performances that leave audiences spellbound. Their energy, stage presence, and ability to connect with their fans make them one of the most sought-after bands in India. They have performed at some of the biggest music festivals in the country, including NH7 Weekender, Bacardi NH7 Weekender, and MTV India Tour..

Merchandise
